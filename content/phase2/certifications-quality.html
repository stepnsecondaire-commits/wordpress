Certifications & Quality for Smart Pet Products Manufacturing

Eviehome is a compliance-first manufacturer. Every electronic smart pet product that leaves our factory in Hefei, China is tested, certified and documented for the destination market. This page lists the certifications we hold, the quality processes we run, and what we can deliver to importers and distributors who need traceable compliance documentation for customs, retail partners and end consumers.

Certifications we hold for smart pet products

Europe and the United Kingdom

  • CE marking: EMC Directive 2014/30/EU and Low Voltage Directive 2014/35/EU for all electrical pet products. Accompanied by a signed Declaration of Conformity (DoC) and an EU authorized representative where required.
  • UKCA marking: post-Brexit conformity mark for the British market.
  • RED (Radio Equipment Directive 2014/53/EU): for WiFi and Bluetooth enabled products (smart feeders, cameras, trackers).
  • REACH compliance: Annex XVII and Candidate List of Substances of Very High Concern. Declarations updated at every REACH revision.
  • ROHS 2 (Directive 2011/65/EU): restriction of hazardous substances in electrical and electronic equipment.
  • WEEE (Directive 2012/19/EU): producer responsibility for electrical and electronic waste. We support WEEE registration by providing weight and material declarations.

North America

  • FCC Part 15: unintentional radiators for all our electronic pet products sold in the United States. Test reports available on request.
  • FCC Part 15 Subpart C: intentional radiators for WiFi, Bluetooth and sub-GHz wireless products.
  • California Prop 65: compliance declaration for pet products sold in California, covering lead, phthalates and BPA exposure risks.
  • IC (Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada): radio certification for Canada for WiFi and Bluetooth enabled models.

Asia Pacific

  • PSE (Japan): Electrical Appliance and Material Safety Law mark for our power adapters and plug-in devices sold in Japan.
  • RCM (Australia and New Zealand): Regulatory Compliance Mark for electrical safety and EMC.
  • KC (South Korea): available on selected models on request.

Factory-level quality systems

  • ISO 9001:2015: quality management system covering our production lines in Hefei.
  • BSCI: Business Social Compliance Initiative audit on social responsibility and working conditions.
  • Food-contact materials compliance: FDA and EU 10/2011 for all plastic and stainless steel parts in contact with pet food or water (feeders, fountains, bowls).

Quality control process

Our quality control runs in three layers on every order:

  1. Incoming component inspection (IQC): every batch of components (plastic parts, PCBs, motors, pumps, power adapters, cables) is inspected on receipt against the approved specification. Non-conforming parts are rejected before they reach the production line.
  2. In-process quality control (IPQC): during assembly, functional tests are performed at key steps (motor rotation, water flow, WiFi pairing, app pairing, battery charging). Defective units are reworked or rejected on the spot.
  3. Outgoing quality control (OQC): 100% function test on every finished unit, plus AQL 2.5 visual and dimensional inspection on a random sample of each production batch per ISO 2859-1 standards.

Third-party inspections we support

On request, we cooperate with third-party inspection bodies at the buyer's cost:

  • SGS
  • Bureau Veritas
  • TUV Rheinland
  • Intertek
  • QIMA (formerly AsiaInspection)

Inspections can be scheduled during production (DUPRO) or before shipment (PSI), and we provide full access to the production line, the finished goods warehouse and the documentation archive.
