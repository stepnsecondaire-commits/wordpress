Shipping & Logistics for Smart Pet Products from China

Shipping bulky consumer electronics across oceans is where many first-time importers get surprised by hidden costs, unexpected delays and customs headaches. At Eviehome we have been exporting smart pet products from China since 2014, and we work with our clients to make the logistics as predictable as the product itself. This page explains how we ship, the Incoterms we support, the lead times you can expect, and the documents we provide for customs clearance in the United States, Europe and other markets.

Incoterms we support

All our quotes are available under the Incoterms you prefer. The three most common for our B2B clients are:

FOB (Free On Board)

We handle all logistics from our factory in Hefei to the loading port in China (typically Ningbo or Shanghai), including inland trucking, export customs clearance, and loading onto the vessel. Your forwarder takes over at the port of origin. FOB is the most flexible and cost-transparent option for experienced importers who already have a preferred freight forwarder.

CIF (Cost, Insurance and Freight)

We handle FOB plus the ocean freight to your named port of destination (Los Angeles, Long Beach, New York, Hamburg, Rotterdam, Antwerp, Le Havre, Felixstowe, Sydney, etc.) and the minimum cargo insurance required by Incoterms 2020. You clear the goods through customs at destination and handle the final trucking to your warehouse. CIF is the easiest option for first-time importers.

DDP (Delivered Duty Paid)

Door-to-door: we handle everything from our factory to your warehouse, including import duties, VAT prepayment (where applicable), customs clearance at destination, and final delivery. DDP is the most convenient option but the price includes a freight forwarder markup. For larger volumes or regular shipments, FOB or CIF almost always comes out cheaper.

Loadability guide by container

If you are sizing your order around a full container load, here are typical loadabilities for our main product categories based on master carton dimensions:

  • 20 ft container (28 CBM usable): approximately 900 to 1 500 automatic cat litter boxes, 2 500 to 4 000 pet feeders, 3 000 to 5 000 pet water fountains depending on the model.
  • 40 ft HQ container (68 CBM usable): approximately 2 200 to 3 600 cat litter boxes, 6 000 to 10 000 pet feeders, 7 000 to 12 000 pet water fountains.
  • LCL (Less than Container Load): available for orders starting at 2 to 3 CBM. We can consolidate with other outbound shipments to reduce your freight cost per unit.

Export documentation we provide

On every shipment, we deliver a complete document pack for your customs broker:

  • Commercial invoice with HS codes
  • Packing list with carton dimensions and gross / net weights
  • Bill of lading (B/L) or sea waybill, or airway bill (AWB) for air freight
  • Certificate of origin (CO or FORM A for countries with preferential trade agreements)
  • CE or UKCA Declaration of Conformity
  • FCC SDoC for shipments to the United States
  • Test reports from accredited labs
  • Fumigation certificate (IPPC ISPM 15) for wood pallets and crates
  • Pro forma invoice before production starts
  • Shipping notice with tracking number 24 hours after departure

HS codes for smart pet products

For reference, here are the most common HS codes we use on export documents for smart pet products. Your customs broker will confirm the exact code applicable in your country:

  • Automatic cat litter box with motor: HS 8509 80 (other electromechanical domestic appliances with self-contained motor)
  • Pet water fountain with pump: HS 8413 70 (centrifugal pumps)
  • Automatic pet feeder (plug-in or battery): HS 8516 79 (other electrothermic appliances of a kind used for domestic purposes)
  • Smart pet camera or WiFi camera: HS 8525 89 (television cameras)
  • GPS pet tracker: HS 9014 20 (instruments and appliances for aeronautical or space navigation)
  • Smart bark collar (electronic training collar): HS 8543 70 (other electrical machines and apparatus, not specified elsewhere)
  • Robot vacuum: HS 8508 11 (vacuum cleaners with self-contained electric motor)
  • Plastic pet bowls and accessories: HS 3924 90 (other household articles of plastics)
