Six-layer safety architecture

The cat safety story is the single most important buyer concern in the category, especially for multi-cat households and households with kittens. This model was designed around a six-layer detection architecture that goes beyond the industry norm:

  • PIR living object detection — passive infrared senses the cat approaching and entering the chamber before the rotation cycle starts
  • Dual infrared sensors at the cabin door — confirms the cat has fully entered or fully exited before the drum moves
  • Four infrared sensors inside the waste chamber — redundant detection of any object or body inside the rotation zone
  • Grating sensor — mechanical interruption stop
  • Four weight sensors — instant halt if a load is detected during movement
  • Hall sensor + child safety lock — physical lock against accidental activation and tampering

This redundancy reduces the residual "cat trapped inside" risk to near zero. It is the kind of spec that survives a 1-star review audit and anchors the premium positioning of a brand sold at USD 599 to 799 retail.

Odor and hygiene system

Odor control is the second-most searched complaint in the automatic litter box category. This model addresses odor through a layered approach:

  • UV-C sterilization inside the waste chamber neutralizes bacteria and limits biofilm growth between cleaning cycles
  • Activated carbon deodorization at the exhaust outlet captures ammonia and volatile sulfur compounds from urine and feces
  • Dual-mode ventilation (automatic timed or manual) actively pulls air from the chamber through the carbon filter instead of relying on passive diffusion
  • Two fragrance cartridge slots (optional) for brands that want to offer a scented consumable subscription SKU alongside the hardware
  • Washable bucket silo — the rotation drum separates completely for deep cleaning every 4 to 6 weeks

The fragrance cartridges are an intentional design for recurring revenue: USD 12 to 18 retail per 4-pack with 70 percent margin, attach rate 25 to 35 percent on hardware buyers.
