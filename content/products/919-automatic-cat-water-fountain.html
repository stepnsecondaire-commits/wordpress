Why 304 stainless steel is the premium choice

304 stainless steel (also known as 18/8 — 18 percent chromium, 8 percent nickel) is the food-grade stainless steel standard used in commercial kitchen equipment and premium consumer products. For a pet water fountain, 304 stainless has four specific advantages over plastic:

  • No biofilm growth: bacteria struggle to adhere to smooth stainless steel the way they cling to scratched or porous plastic. This means lower bacterial load in the drinking water between cleanings.
  • No chemical leaching: no concern about BPA, phthalates, or plastic off-flavors that some cats detect and reject
  • Scratch resistance: cats paw and scratch the bowl. Plastic accumulates scratches; stainless steel does not
  • Temperature stability: stays cooler than plastic in warm environments, which cats prefer
  • Dishwasher-safe: most owners put the stainless components in the dishwasher for weekly deep cleaning

Capacity and household fit

3-liter capacity is the sweet spot for:

  • Single cat households: refill every 6 to 10 days, which matches typical owner routine
  • Two-cat households: refill every 4 to 6 days
  • Single small dog households: refill every 3 to 5 days depending on breed size

For 3+ cat households or large dog households, we recommend higher capacity models (4.5L+) to reduce refill friction. Smaller households can use the 2.2L variant if space is limited.
