Compact automatic cat litter box built around odor control

This model is designed around a single dominant feature: odor neutralization. It is the product we recommend for brands targeting apartment dwellers, multi-cat households sensitive to smell, and markets where the litter box has to live in the main living area rather than a hidden utility room.

The compact footprint (58 x 55 cm) is deliberately smaller than our flagship models to fit apartment placement, and the product retails typically between USD 249 and USD 399. Tuya Smart 2.4G WiFi integration is standard, which gives brands a fast path to white-label deployment without custom firmware engineering.

Lons odor purification system

The core differentiator of this model is the Lons odor purification technology built into the waste chamber. The system neutralizes ammonia, mercaptan and volatile sulfur compounds that drive the "cat urine smell" rather than just masking them with fragrance. This is the technology that justifies the "odor-free" product naming and makes the marketing claim defensible under buyer scrutiny.

For brands that want to emphasize the health benefits: ammonia exposure from a conventional uncovered litter box can reach 5 to 10 parts per million in the immediate vicinity after a cat urinates, which irritates human and feline respiratory systems. The Lons system drops these peaks by 70 to 85 percent in controlled testing.

Why the compact design matters

Most premium automatic litter boxes are large: 60 to 75 cm wide and 65+ cm tall. In a 40 to 60 square-meter apartment, that footprint is a problem. This model is specifically tuned for apartment buyers:

  • 58 x 55 x 29 cm unit dimensions — fits in closets, bathrooms and under counters where larger models cannot
  • Lower profile — the 29 cm height is below most cabinets and furniture
  • 10 kg net weight — easy for a single adult to move for cleaning or relocation
  • 12 L waste capacity — adequate for a single cat with weekly emptying, or 2 cats with twice-weekly emptying

The compromise is a smaller drum capacity (not specified in the standard spec because it is geared for compactness, not capacity). For multi-cat households with 3+ cats, our 76L and 68L models are a better fit.

Certifications and compliance

Standard package: CE + RED + EMC for EU, FCC for US, ROHS, REACH with SVHC screening. The Lons odor purification module uses food-grade and skin-contact-safe materials certified under FDA 21 CFR and EU 10/2011 for the air-contact components.

The WiFi module is the Tuya-standard ESP32 variant with pre-existing FCC and CE authorization, which simplifies the regulatory path for custom-branded units.

Logistics

Package dimensions 61 x 57 x 33 cm. Net weight 10 kg, gross weight 13 kg. A 20-foot container holds approximately 130 to 150 units; a 40-foot holds 280 to 320 units depending on stacking. The compact footprint means per-container unit count is higher than our flagship 76L models, which improves per-unit shipping economics.

Sea freight is the recommended mode. Transit times: 18 to 25 days to US West Coast, 30 to 40 days to US East Coast, 30 to 40 days to EU ports.
